    Manage document access

    Prerequisites:

    • Give an untitled document a name before it can be shared.

    To manage document access, do the following:

    1. Open the document in Google Docs.
    2. Click Share.
    3. In the Share dialog, enter the email address you want to share with.
    4. From the list, select role:
      • Viewer
      • Commenter
      • Editor
    5. Enter notification message.
    6. Optional: To skip notifying people, clear Notify people box.
    7. Optional: Click Copy link to copy the document link.
    8. Click Send.

    The document will be shared to the entered emails.
